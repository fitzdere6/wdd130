About Us

Welcome to Goin' Raftin', your premier rafting adventure company based in the heart of Eastern Idaho! Nestled among the rugged beauty of the Rocky Mountains and the serene flow of the Snake River, our family-owned business is dedicated to providing unforgettable rafting experiences. Founded by outdoor enthusiasts who grew up exploring the pristine rivers and breathtaking landscapes of Idaho, Goin' Raftin' offers expertly guided tours that cater to both thrill-seekers and nature lovers. Whether you're navigating the exhilarating rapids of the Salmon River or enjoying a peaceful float down the Henry's Fork, our knowledgeable guides ensure a safe, fun, and educational adventure. Join us at Goin' Raftin' and discover why Eastern Idaho is the perfect destination for your next rafting journey!
